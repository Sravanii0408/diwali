HAPPY DIWALI

Diwali, also known as the "Festival of Lights," is a major annual Hindu festival that is celebrated in India and other parts of South Asia. The festival typically occurs between mid-October and mid-November and lasts for five days. Diwali marks the triumph of good over evil, as well > as the beginning of the Hindu New Year. During the festival, people light candles, diyas (oil lamps), and colorful lights to illuminate their homes and public spaces. They also decorate their homes with rangolis (intricate floor designs made with colored powders), wear new clothes, exchange gifts, and prepare special festive meals.
